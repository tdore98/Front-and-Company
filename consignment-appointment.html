PLEASE READ ALL INFORMATION BELOW BEFORE BOOKING AN APPOINTMENT:

Consignment items are now accepted at 3742 Main Street, the space adjacent to our main store. Select an appointment time to reduce wait time and maximize social distancing.

Before your appointment, please clean your in style and in good condition items (no stains, holes, pilling, missing buttons, etc.) and leave the items in bags untouched for at least 24 hours. Maximum of 2 bags per appointment please. Please do not bring items on hangers!

We are currently accepting SPRING AND EARLY SUMMER. Some examples of what we are looking for are:

  • Light-weight, sleeveless or short-sleeved tops and blouses
  • Oversized, light-coloured shirts
  • Shorts and sandals
  • Lightweight, summery dresses
  • Denim jackets
  • Fabrics such as silk, linen and cotton
  • Pastel and brightly coloured activewear

We are NOT currently accepting:

  • Dark, fall colours and heavy fabrics (wool, cashmere, tweed)
  • Heavy-weight denim and pants
  • Boots
  • Puffer jackets and wool coats
  • Heavy knitwear such as cardigans and sweaters
  • Winter accessories (ie: scarves and toques)

If you have any questions regarding this, please give us a call at 604-879-8431 ex 2 or email at consignment@frontandcompany.com
